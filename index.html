First-time setup

Paste the published CSV URL of your Google Sheet below. Film data is stored directly in your sheet — no API calls are made when visitors load the site.

How to get the Google Sheet CSV URL:
In Google Sheets go to File → Share → Publish to web, choose Entire document and Comma-separated values (.csv), then click Publish. Paste the URL here.

Required sheet columns (exact names in row 1):
imdb_url   actresses   title   year   poster   rating   genre   country   synopsis
Columns C–I are filled automatically by the Apps Script — you only enter A and B.
