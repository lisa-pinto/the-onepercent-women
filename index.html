The6%

Men make up only 68-71% of all lead or co-lead roles in feature films, but when it comes to characters above 50, that number rises to an overwhelming 93%. This site is dedicated to the 6% of older leading characters played by women. Whether stereotypical or not. These films are carried by their lead actresses showing us that visibility is not just necessary for the demographic but valuable entertainment for everyone.
